# Examples

The following examples are all included in `ModTemplate` and can be built and run out of the box. They are intentionally simple and focused on a single mechanic each — use them as starting points for your own content.

All examples share a gold theme to keep the localization and logic easy to follow at a glance.

| Category | Example | What it demonstrates |
|----------|---------|---------------------|
| [Potions](potions.md) | Drop of Gold | Simple potion that grants gold with a VFX splash |
| [Potions](potions.md) | Gold Paint | Potion that applies a custom power to enemies |
| [Cards](cards.md) | Coin Flip | Card with RNG, dynamic variables, upgrade behavior, and banter text |
| [Relics](relics.md) | Gold Armor | Relic with a turn-start hook, conditional logic, and multiple dynamic variables |
| [Powers](powers.md) | Made of Gold | Debuff power that triggers after damage is received |
| [Events](events.md) | The Gold Coin Room | Multi-page event with branching choices and state transitions |
| [Ancients](ancients.md) | Gold Guy | Ancient event that offers a choice of relics |
