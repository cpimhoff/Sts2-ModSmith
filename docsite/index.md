Hello! Welcome to the world of Slay the Spire 2 modding. ModSmith is a library to help you get started quickly and start adding cool nonsense into the game.

## Installation

ModSmith itself has no content, but provides tooling your other mods may require to run. To install ModSmith, [follow these instructions](./docs/setup/install.md).

If you are just playing with mods, then that's it.
If you are _developing_ mods, then you will have to [set up your development environment](./docs/setup/guided.md).
