Examples

The following examples are all included in ModTemplate and can be built and run out of the box. They are intentionally simple and focused on a single mechanic each — use them as starting points for your own content.

All examples share a gold theme to keep the localization and logic easy to follow at a glance.

Category Example What it demonstrates
Potions Drop of Gold Simple potion that grants gold with a VFX splash
Potions Gold Paint Potion that applies a custom power to enemies
Cards Coin Flip Card with RNG, dynamic variables, upgrade behavior, and banter text
Relics Gold Armor Relic with a turn-start hook, conditional logic, and multiple dynamic variables
Powers Made of Gold Debuff power that triggers after damage is received
Events The Gold Coin Room Multi-page event with branching choices and state transitions
Ancients Gold Guy Ancient event that offers a choice of relics
